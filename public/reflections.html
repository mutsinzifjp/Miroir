Reflections

This section is not neutral. These are moments when I pause and see the echoes inside me. Raw thoughts, visual truths, and whispers that don’t fit into data or categories — but still matter.

Where Do I Go Now?
Religion taught me that being different was evil.
School taught me nothing real about gender — just words to memorize.
Media taught me rainbow meant freedom, but never let me feel it.

I was trained to fear difference, to mock softness, to forget nuance.
But now… I stand unsure, still unhealed, still becoming.
Where do I go now? Who do I trust?

I still don’t know.
But I know this: I won’t become what made me silent.
