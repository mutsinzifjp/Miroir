Reflections

This section is a living, evolving room where thoughts breathe. It holds philosophical, emotional, and spiritual reflections— especially those that challenge systems of conditioning and reclaim human dignity. These are not final truths. They are openings. Entry points. Mirrors.

Systems Taught Me to Hate Difference

I was taught to fear and hate what I didn't understand. Religion made me believe that LGBTQ+ was the highway to hell. School never let me live the concepts of gender roles— only repeat them in exams. I was denied the truth, the nuance, the experience.

The media told me freedom comes with a rainbow but refused to let me breathe in that freedom. It told me that breaking gender norms equals becoming deviant. I lived in this fog, built by systems that never asked who I am—only who I must pretend to be.

Now, I ask: who shall I believe? Where shall I go? The answer comes gently: I am still becoming. This is not shame. It’s awakening.
