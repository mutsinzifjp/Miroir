My Mirror

This space holds my Butoh performances and opera renditions. Each piece is not an act, it is a message, a question, a reflection. Here, I perform truth with my body. Through Butoh, I reclaim what was silenced. Through opera, I shatter the gender scripts assigned to voice and form.

Connect with me: LinkedIn | Instagram

Carmen: Performed in femme voice and attire, this is a rebellion against rigid masculinity. Expect confrontation, seduction, and satire of gendered power.

Reclaiming Lost Childhood: A visual cry for what was stolen. Expect vulnerability, resistance, and the sacred rage of innocence rediscovered.

O Fortuna: The fury of fate and divine injustice. Expect raw physicality and sacred chaos.

Der Holle: Descent into society’s judgment. Expect dark satire and confrontations with moral hypocrisy.

Lacrimosa: A mourning song for silenced souls. Expect sacred grief and resilience through embodied lamentation.

Crossing the Threshold: A passage ritual. Expect transformation and a goodbye to the past self that conformed.
