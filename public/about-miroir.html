About Miroir

Miroir is a living project that challenges how society looks at truth, gender, and resistance. It’s not about trends or approval. It's a space to reflect, confront, and remember. Every action, word, and silence here is intentional.

Truth

Miroir centers personal and political truths that are usually silenced or denied. It reflects reality, even when it’s uncomfortable.

Rebellion

This is not a neutral space. Miroir resists conformity and exposes the systems that force people into roles, shame, or invisibility.

Memory

Every story here carries memory—of trauma, of beauty, of resistance. This space does not forget what society wants erased.
