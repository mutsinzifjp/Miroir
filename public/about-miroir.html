About Miroir

Miroir is not just a website, it is a living, breathing archive of resistance, identity, and truth. Born from lived experiences and expressive acts like Butoh, opera, and philosophical resistance, Miroir exists to reflect what society often erases: the complexity of gender, the courage of personal truth, and the power of reclaiming our own narratives.

Through multimedia performances, public provocations, and deep storytelling, Miroir stands as a hub for those who defy being boxed, those who see rebellion not as destruction, but as rebirth. It is for the femmeboys, for the misread, for the violated and the victorious.

Every page is a threshold. Every voice is a wound and a weapon. And every visit is a confrontation with a world that must change.
