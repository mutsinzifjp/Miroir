Truth Tools – Part One

Speak the Truth, Stay Alive
For creators who refuse to be censored, erased, or reshaped to please the algorithm.

They can hide your post. They can ban your account. But if you’re wise, they’ll never erase your voice.

This is not a trend. This is a survival manual for truth-tellers in a system that fears honesty. For the small creators who don’t create to impress — but to wake up the world. This is your mirror, your compass, and your sword.

When I started to speak my truth online, I became my only audience.

I was the only one liking my own posts. I feared my own voice. I rewrote captions five times to make them less “offensive.” I tried pleasing. I tried silence.

But silence never saved me. So I chose truth. Not perfection. Not applause. Just truth.

I didn’t have a team. I didn’t know how to code. But I built this entire site by feeding questions to ChatGPT, dragging shapes in Canva, and refusing to give up.

You don’t need to be a tech genius to resist. You just need to keep walking — barefoot if you have to.

You are not alone. You’re just early.

Truth in this age is not silenced by bullets. It’s silenced by invisibility.

What gets shadowbanned:

  • Posts with words like “resist,” “Palestine,” “colonial,” “rape,” “queer truth,” “free thinkers”
  • Hashtags like #wakeup, #decolonize, #feministkilljoy
  • Repeated links to activist sites
  • Art that mocks politicians, religion, systems

How to stay loud but smart:

  • Use poetry to hide truth in metaphor.
  • Post art instead of rants. (It slips through filters.)
  • Avoid banned hashtags; use unique ones.
  • Reupload videos across platforms.
  • Always download your content offline.

Survival Tips:

  • Use Archive.org to mirror your work.
  • Use Notion to write, backup, and store offline.
  • Create backups on USB or drive weekly.
  • Own your domain. Never trust only Instagram.

I created everything you see here without knowing how to code.

Here’s my toolkit for truth-tellers:

Tool Purpose Link
ChatGPT Ask anything: design, code, strategy chat.openai.com
Canva Make graphics, pages, posters canva.com
Tally.so Build simple forms or surveys tally.so
Archive.org Save your work in the cloud, forever archive.org
Notion Write, plan, and save offline notion.so
GitHub + Vercel Free website hosting github.com, vercel.com

You don't need to master them all — just start with what scares you the least.

A World Colonized Without Chains

The modern world is so colonized it doesn’t even see the chains — because the chains are now invisible: in ads, beliefs, silence, likes.

We live in a system where:

  • Profits come before people.
  • Truth is treated like stale beer.
  • People censor themselves before being censored.

You will be called “too much.” You will be labeled “crazy,” “angry,” “ungrateful.” That means you’re on the right track.

They told me to quiet down, spiritualize it, filter it. I chose instead to unfilter, to bleed where I was once bandaged.

Let your content scream the truths your society pretends not to hear.
